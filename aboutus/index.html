01ERC ABAY-ABAY CUSTOMS BROKERAGE

Your First Step to Worry-Free Trading

A duly accredited and authorized Customs Brokerage firm with the Philippine Bureau of Customs. The company’s operational team is headed by Mr. Emman Reyan C. Abay-abay, a licensed customs broker with vast experience stemmed from customs broking since the 1990’s from his father and his firm, Mr. Leonardo R. Abay-abay of LRA Customs Brokerage.

We have a dedicated team who understands the import and export industry and thinks outside the box. We partner with businesses from various industries in managing all aspects of international trade; ensuring goods reach its destination on time and in good condition.

ERC Abay-abay Customs Brokerage prides itself on delivering excellent results and customer service. From start to finish of the customs clearance process, the same Customs Broker who evaluates, classifies, and generates your entry will also get in touch with you to personally explain why they have applied the specific customs tariffs, concessions, valuation and relevant legislation to your consignment. Our team will seek the best available option for our clients, while taking into consideration the overall responsibility to government regulations and audit processes.
